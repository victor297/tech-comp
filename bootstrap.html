The Sam Technology

The Sam Tech museum keeps more than 500 robotic machines. Built on Manhattan's Lower East Side in 1863, this tenement apartment building was home to nearly 7000 working class immigrants. Ironpaper is the web master of the Tenement Museum and built a new online store for the Museum that integrates with Microsoft's RMS inventory system.
